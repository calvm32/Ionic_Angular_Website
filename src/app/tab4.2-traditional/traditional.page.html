Traditional Art

Most of the art pictured was made from 2023-present. My motivation behind this art is to express my emotions without worrying about the quality or quantity of what I produce. I have had no formal art instruction, so any techniques I use are credited to the artists I draw inspiration from. I frequently use several different mediums on one piece, because I find layering different mediums is the best way to engage and give voices to different parts of my brain.
