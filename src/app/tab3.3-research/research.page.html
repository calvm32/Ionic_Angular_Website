Galois Theory Research

My current research is using Artin-Schreier presentations to classify Galois extensions of degree {{ p6 }} and {{ p5 }} and maximal nilpotency class over a field of characteristic p, for some prime p. From May 2023-24, I was funded by the NASA Nebraska Space Grant. My profile is featured on the NASA Nebraska Space Grant website here.
On May 31st, 2024, I presented at the annual Hopf Algebras and Galois Module Theory Conference in Omaha, Nebraska. A recording of my presentation and the poster can be found here.
