Constructions

Mathematical objects that I made in various websites to highlight their visual aspects.

Great Rhombic Triacontahedron
Using Geogebra 3d graphing software and information on the Polytope Wiki, I made this Great Rhombic Triacontahedron. My graph can be found here. The colors are placed symmetrically to show how the different rhombi fit together.
Volume of a Tetrahedron
Using Geogebra 3d graphing software, I made an interactive visual "proof" that the volume of any tetrahedron is 1/3 the volume of its circumscribed rectangular prism. My graph can be found here.
Colorful Torus
Using Geogebra 3d graphing software and equations for the graph of a torus, I made this rainbow spiral torus. My graph can be found here. The colors form a gradient automatically as the circles revolve around the center of the torus.
Examining Field Extensions
These are representations of certain field extensions used in my current research. The graphs were made in LaTeX with the xymatrix package.
